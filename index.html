About The Book & Previous Stories

As a child, Ben was haunted by monsters in the closet. As an adult, he’s convinced he'd imagined them, until he conducts a physics experiment that peels back a terrifying layer of reality… and he discovers the monsters are very real. Ben's discovery gives him a dangerous power he can't control. The monsters want it. So does a powerful corporation that’s willing to do anything to tap into a fraction of what Ben's found: bridges to quantum worlds. Some of those worlds hold painful secrets and a burning need for vengeance. Even if Ben manages to learn how to control his power before it falls into the wrong hands, he faces an impossible choice. Justice… or humanity's continued existence. In the spirit of Blake Crouch’s Dark Matter and Ted Kosmatka’s Flicker Men, this mind-bending hard sci-fi thriller shakes the foundation of reality.

Physics Ideas for Science Fiction Stories

In this captivating book, explore a collection of mind-bending concepts that push the boundaries of science fiction. Delve into a world where the stability of the sun is at stake, threatening the existence of life on Earth. Discover a civilization from Earth's ancient past, possessing advanced knowledge but lacking interest in saving humanity. Follow the journey of one individual who dares to persuade these enigmatic beings to aid humankind. Immerse yourself in a unique existence where the absence of traditional senses is compensated by perceiving the world through others' eyes and ears. Witness the extraordinary possibilities that unfold as this individual grows and navigates adulthood. Embark on a quest to send a crucial message to the past, exploring alternatives to tachyons and seeking particles that defy the limits of time. Lastly, step into a special room that allows time travel to the future, enabling the experience of a full day while mere seconds pass outside. With these compelling ideas and many more, this concise book invites you to unlock the potential for extraordinary science fiction narratives.

What's inside the book

Exploring Quantum Mechanics

Delve into the fascinating world of quantum mechanics, where the rules of traditional physics are challenged, and quantum entanglement takes center stage. Unravel the mysteries of entangled particles, superposition, and quantum teleportation as we journey through the complex landscape of quantum worlds.

Mind-Bending Concepts

Brace yourself for mind-bending concepts that push the boundaries of our understanding of reality. Encounter parallel universes, time dilation, and the intriguing concept of the entangled man. Prepare to have your perception of the universe shattered and your imagination set free.

Compelling Characters

Meet a diverse cast of characters who navigate the quantum realms. From brilliant scientists grappling with the implications of entanglement to everyday individuals thrust into extraordinary circumstances, each character adds depth and emotion to the narrative, making their journeys through quantum worlds all the more captivating.

Ethical Dilemmas and Consequences

Explore the moral and ethical implications of manipulating quantum phenomena. As characters grapple with the power and consequences of entanglement, profound questions emerge. What are the limits of our control over the quantum realm, and how far should we go in harnessing its potential? Prepare for thought-provoking dilemmas that challenge your own ethical compass.

Thrilling Plot Twists

Brace yourself for unexpected twists and turns that keep you on the edge of your seat. As the boundaries of quantum worlds blur, mysteries deepen, and secrets unravel, the plot takes exhilarating twists that will leave you guessing until the final pages. Prepare for a thrilling journey that merges cutting-edge science with gripping storytelling.

Bridging Science and Imagination

"Quantum Worlds and The Entangled Man" seamlessly weaves together scientific principles with imaginative storytelling. While exploring the fascinating concepts of quantum physics, the narrative invites readers to ponder the limits of human understanding, the nature of consciousness, and the interconnectedness of the universe. Get ready for a unique blend of scientific wonder and literary escapism.

What inspired you to pursue writing?

I decided to pursue writing due to my lifelong passion for both quantum physics and storytelling. The mind-boggling concepts of quantum mechanics have always fascinated me, and I wanted to share that fascination with readers in a way that combines scientific accuracy with imaginative storytelling. The desire to explore the complexities of entanglement, parallel universes, and the implications of quantum phenomena on human existence drove me to create a narrative that challenges readers' perceptions of reality while keeping them engrossed in a thrilling plot. Ultimately, I was inspired to write this book to ignite curiosity, spark discussions, and provide readers with an unforgettable journey through the enigmatic world of quantum physics.

Know More About Author

Mark R.A Shegelski - Professor Emeritus

I am a Professor Emeritus in physics at the University of Northern British Columbia, having joined UNBC as a founding member in physics in 1994. Through Scroll Press I have previously published a book of 14 linked science fiction stories, Remembering the Future, available as an Ebook on Amazon. I have one hundred physics publications, many of which address the fundamental ideas in my novel Quantum Worlds and the Entangled Man (co-authored with Joseph Halden). With Professor Emeritus Edward Lozowski, I developed the first theory to account for the motion of curling rocks (“Pivot-Slide” theory, published in Canadian Journal of Physics (2016) and Cold Regions Science and Technology (2018)). I enjoy music (Moody Blues, Jean-Michel Jarre, Kitaro, Marillion and more). I am currently working on a short book, Physics Ideas for Science Fiction Stories. I live with my wife in British Columbia.
